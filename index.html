The Problem

Most companies don't fail because they lack data. They fail because execution breaks down.

The loop looks the same in every plant we walk into.

A supplier misses a shipment. The team scrambles. The customer gets an excuse. Repeat.

Most companies have the data they need. What they're missing is the operating discipline between internal teams and external suppliers. That's where we focus.

  • 02Inventory that's simultaneously too high and too low.
  • 03A planning process that nobody trusts.
  • 04Tariff exposure with no backup plan.
  • 05A team burning out from heroic recoveries.
